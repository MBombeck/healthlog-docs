## Getting Started After Installation

### 1. Create Your Account

Open HealthLog in your browser and create an account. You will be prompted to register a **Passkey** (fingerprint, Face ID, or security key). The first account automatically receives admin privileges.

:::note
Passkeys are device-bound. Register a Passkey on every device you want to log in from (phone, laptop, tablet).
:::

### 2. Complete the Onboarding

After registration, the onboarding wizard walks you through the basic setup:

- **Height** — used for BMI calculation
- **Date of birth** — for age-dependent reference ranges
- **Sex** — for sex-specific normal values

You can change these later in the settings.

### 3. Log Your First Measurement

Tap the **plus icon** in the navigation bar and pick a category:

- **Weight** — optionally with body fat percentage
- **Blood Pressure** — systolic, diastolic, heart rate
- **Sleep** — duration and quality
- **Activity** — type, duration, intensity
- **Mood** — daily rating with an optional note

All values are instantly visualized on the dashboard with trend lines and statistics.

### 4. Set Up Medications

Under **Medications**, you can add your current prescriptions:

- Name, active ingredient, and dosage
- Scheduled times (e.g., 08:00 AM, 08:00 PM)
- As-needed medications (no fixed schedule)
- Start date and optional end date

HealthLog tracks your compliance automatically and shows adherence statistics.

### 5. Configure Notifications

Set up at least one notification channel to receive reminders:

| Channel | Setup |
|---|---|
| **Web Push** | Grant the browser permission — works without any external services |
| **Telegram** | Enter your bot token and chat ID in the settings |
| **ntfy** | Configure the server URL and topic |

Reminders are sent for medication doses and optional measurement reminders.

### 6. Optional Integrations

**Withings Sync**

Connect your Withings account under **Settings > Integrations**. Supported devices:

- Withings Body+ / Body Comp (weight, body fat)
- Withings BPM Connect (blood pressure, heart rate)

Measurements are imported automatically — no manual entry needed.

**AI Insights (OpenAI)**

HealthLog can analyze your health data with OpenAI to deliver personalized insights. Enter your own API key under **Settings > AI**. No API key is included — it is strictly BYOK (Bring Your Own Key).

:::tip
Even without the AI integration, HealthLog provides detailed statistics, trend analysis, and reference range comparisons right on the dashboard.
:::

## Tips for Daily Use

- **Install the PWA** — Open HealthLog in your mobile browser and select "Add to Home Screen." The app then runs just like a native application.
- **Use the quick entry** — The plus icon is accessible from any page. Logging a weight entry takes less than 5 seconds.
- **Check the achievements** — Under **Profile > Achievements**, you will find 38 unlockable achievements for consistent tracking.
- **Export medical reports** — Before your next doctor's appointment, generate a PDF export with historical data and charts under **Reports**.
